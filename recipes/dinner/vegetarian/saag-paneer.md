## Ingredients

- 14 oz paneer, cubed
- 1 lb fresh spinach (or 10 oz frozen, thawed and squeezed dry)
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
- 1 can diced tomatoes (14 oz)
- 1/2 cup heavy cream or coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- 1 teaspoon coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne
- salt to taste
- basmati rice and naan for serving

## Instructions

1. Blanch spinach in boiling water 1 minute. Drain and blend until smooth. Set aside.
2. Heat butter in a large skillet. Add cumin seeds and let sizzle 30 seconds.
3. Add onion and cook 8 minutes until golden. Add garlic and ginger.
4. Stir in garam masala, coriander, turmeric, and cayenne. Cook 1 minute.
5. Add diced tomatoes and cook 5 minutes.
6. Stir in blended spinach. Simmer 5 minutes.
7. Add cream and paneer. Heat through 3-4 minutes. Season and serve.

## Notes

- Paneer is available at King Soopers in the cheese section
- Pan-fry the paneer cubes in butter first for a crispier texture
- Substitute firm tofu for a vegan version
