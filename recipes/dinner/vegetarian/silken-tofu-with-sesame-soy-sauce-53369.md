# Summary

Silken Tofu with Sesame Soy Sauce from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 16 ounces Silken Tofu
- 3  tablespoons Soy Sauce
- 2 tablespoons Chinese Sesame Sauce
- 1 sliced Scallions
- 2 tsp Sesame Seed

## Instructions

1. Prepare the tofu:.
2. Drain the tofu and gently remove it from its packaging onto a large plate.
3. Carefully slice the tofu into 1/2-inch slabs widthwise.
4. With the palm of your hands, gently push the sliced tofu sidewise so that they fan out over the plate.
5. Garnish with toppings and serve:.
6. Drizzle the soy sauce and sesame sauce on top.
7. Then garnish it with scallions and sesame seeds, and serve.
8. Tightly cover leftovers and refrigerate for up to 2 days.
9. You can enjoy it cold straight out of the fridge or you can reheat it by microwaving in 30-second increments until warmed through.
