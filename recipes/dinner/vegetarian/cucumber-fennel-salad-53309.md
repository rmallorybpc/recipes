# Summary

Cucumber & fennel salad from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 1 large Cucumber
- 1 tsp Sugar
- 1 sliced Fennel Bulb
- 150ml Sour Cream
- Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1 tablespoon White Wine Vinegar
- Bunch Dill

## Instructions

1. Put cucumber in a sieve.
2. Sprinkle with 1 tsp salt and the sugar, then leave for 10 mins.
3. Add fennel.
4. Mix soured cream, lemon juice, vinegar and dill, then season with black pepper and add to fennel mix.
