## Ingredients

- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 carrots, thinly sliced
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1/4 cup water
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- cooked rice for serving
- sesame seeds for garnish

## Instructions

1. Whisk soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, and water for the sauce.
2. Heat oil in a wok over high heat until smoking.
3. Add carrots and broccoli. Stir fry 3 minutes.
4. Add peppers, snap peas, mushrooms, and onion. Stir fry 2 minutes.
5. Add garlic and ginger. Cook 30 seconds.
6. Pour sauce over vegetables. Toss until sauce thickens and coats everything.
7. Serve over rice with sesame seeds.

## Notes

- High heat is essential for a proper stir fry — do not reduce it
- Cut all vegetables before you start — the cooking goes fast
- Add tofu or chicken for a protein version
