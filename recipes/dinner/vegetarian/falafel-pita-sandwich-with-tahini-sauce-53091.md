# Summary

Falafel Pita Sandwich with Tahini Sauce from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 12 Falafel
- 1/4 cup Tahini
- 1/4 cup Water
- 2 tablespoons Lemon Juice
- 2 cloves Garlic
- 1/4 tsp Paprika
- 6 Pita Bread
- 1 Lettuce
- 1 Tomato
- 1/2 Cucumber
- 1 Dill Pickles
- 1/4 Red Onions
- 3 tsp Harissa Spice

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Place falafel on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through, 8 to 10 minutes.
4. While falafel bake, whisk tahini, water, lemon juice, garlic, and paprika together in a bowl.
5. Cut about 1 inch from the top of each pita to form a pocket.
6. Add 2 falafel to each pita with equal amounts lettuce, tomato, cucumber, pickle, and red onion.
7. Drizzle each with about 1 tablespoon tahini sauce and some harissa.
