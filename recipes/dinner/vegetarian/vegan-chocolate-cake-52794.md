# Summary

Vegan Chocolate Cake from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cup Self-raising Flour
- 1/2 cup coco sugar
- 1/3 cup raw cacao
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 2 flax eggs
- 1/2 cup almond milk
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 cup boiling water

## Instructions

1. Simply mix all dry ingredients with wet ingredients and blend altogether.
2. Bake for 45 min on 180 degrees.
3. Decorate with some melted vegan chocolate.
