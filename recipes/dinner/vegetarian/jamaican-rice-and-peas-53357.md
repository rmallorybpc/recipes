# Summary

Jamaican Rice and Peas from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Rice
- 380g Coconut Milk
- 450g Kidney Beans
- 1 cup Water
- 2 tsp Kosher Salt
- 1/2 tsp Ground Allspice
- 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
- 3 Spring Onions
- 2 sprigs Thyme
- 1 Scotch Bonnet

## Instructions

1. Equipment.
2. Dutch oven.
3. Add rice, coconut milk, kidney beans, water, kosher salt, allspice, and black pepper to a large pot and stir until combined.
4. Lay green onions, thyme and Scotch bonnet pepper on top.
5. Bring mixture to a boil.
6. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and cover with a lid.
7. Allow to cook covered for 18 minutes over low heat, then remove from heat.
8. Leave the lid on for an additional 5 minutes.
9. Open the lid and remove the green onion, Scotch bonnet pepper, and thyme.
10. Fluff the rice and peas with a fork.
11. Serve and enjoy.
