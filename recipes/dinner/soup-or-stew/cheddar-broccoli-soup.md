## Ingredients

- 4 cups broccoli florets, chopped
- 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, shredded
- 3 cups chicken or vegetable broth
- 2 cups whole milk or half-and-half
- 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- salt and pepper to taste
- crusty bread for serving

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Cook onion 5 minutes.
2. Add garlic and flour. Cook 2 minutes.
3. Gradually whisk in broth and milk. Bring to a simmer.
4. Add broccoli and carrots. Simmer 15 minutes until broccoli is very tender.
5. Use an immersion blender to blend half the soup for a creamy-chunky texture.
6. Add mustard powder, cayenne, salt, and pepper.
7. Remove from heat. Stir in cheese until melted.
8. Serve with crusty bread.

## Notes

- Do not boil after adding cheese — it will become grainy
- Shred your own cheese for better melting
- Add a swirl of cream on top when serving for presentation
