## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp Olive Oil
- 2 chopped Onions
- 2 sticks Celery
- 300g Carrots
- 500g Potatoes
- 4 Bay Leaf
- 5 tblsp Tomato Puree
- 2 tblsp Sugar
- 2 tblsp White Vinegar
- 1½ kg Chopped Tomatoes
- 500g Passata
- 3 Vegetable Stock Cube
- 400ml Whole Milk

## Instructions

1. Cook onion in butter and oil 7 minutes. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
2. Add tomatoes, broth, sugar, and basil. Simmer 15 minutes.
3. Use an immersion blender to purée until completely smooth.
4. Stir in cream. Season with salt and pepper.
5. For grilled cheese: butter the outside of each bread slice.
6. Cook in a skillet over medium heat 3-4 minutes per side until golden and cheese melts.
7. Serve soup with grilled cheese for dipping.

## Notes

- Canned San Marzano tomatoes make the best tomato soup
- A swirl of cream on top makes a beautiful presentation
- Add a grilled cheese crouton cut into cubes on top of the soup
