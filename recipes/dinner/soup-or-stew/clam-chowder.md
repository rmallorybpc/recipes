## Ingredients

- 1½ kg Clams
- 50g Butter
- 150g Bacon
- 1 finely chopped Onion
- sprigs of fresh Thyme
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 tbls Plain Flour
- 150ml Milk
- 150ml Double Cream
- 2 medium Potatoes
- Chopped Parsley

## Instructions

1. Cook bacon in a large pot until crispy. Remove and leave 1 tablespoon fat.
2. Add onion and celery. Cook 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
3. Stir in butter and flour. Cook 2 minutes.
4. Add potatoes, reserved clam juice, and broth. Simmer 15-20 minutes until potatoes are tender.
5. Stir in clams, heavy cream, and thyme. Simmer 5 minutes. Do not boil after adding cream.
6. Season with salt and white pepper.
7. Serve topped with bacon, chives, and oyster crackers.

## Notes

- For a thicker chowder mash some potatoes against the pot
- Use fresh clams when in season for superior flavor
- Keeps refrigerated for 3 days - reheat gently
