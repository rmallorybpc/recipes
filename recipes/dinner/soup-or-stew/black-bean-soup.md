## Ingredients

- 3 cans black beans, drained and rinsed (reserve 1 can liquid)
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
- 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
- 2 teaspoons cumin
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- juice of 1 lime
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste
- sour cream, avocado, cilantro, and lime for serving

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook onion and bell pepper 6 minutes.
2. Add garlic, jalapeno, cumin, paprika, and chili powder. Cook 1 minute.
3. Add black beans, reserved bean liquid, and broth. Simmer 15 minutes.
4. Use an immersion blender to partially blend - leave about half the beans whole.
5. Stir in lime juice and adjust seasoning.
6. Serve topped with sour cream, avocado, cilantro, and extra lime.

## Notes

- An entirely pantry-based recipe
- Adding smoked chorizo makes this a heartier non-vegetarian version
- Freezes perfectly for up to 3 months
