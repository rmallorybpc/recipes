# Summary

Jamaican Cornmeal Porridge from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups Water
- 380g Coconut Milk
- 1 cup Fine Yellow Cornmeal
- 1 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
- 1/2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp Ground Nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp Ground Allspice
- 1/2 cup Sweetened Condensed Milk

## Instructions

1. Add 2 ½ cups water, 1 can coconut milk to a 4 QT heavy bottomed pot.
2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
3. Meanwhile, add 1 cup cornmeal and 1 ½ cup water to a large mixing cup and whisk until smooth.
4. Once the pot begins to boil, whisk in cornmeal mixture and continue to whisk for about 1 minute, ensuring there are no lumps.
5. Reduce heat to low and cover with a tight fitting lid.
6. Cook over low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
7. When there is about 5 minutes left, stir in vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and all-spice.
8. Remove from heat and sweeten with condensed milk.
9. Serve and enjoy.
