## Ingredients

- 2 cans black beans (15 oz each), drained and rinsed
- 10 flour or corn tortillas
- 2 cans red enchilada sauce (10 oz each)
- 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 can diced green chiles (4 oz)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup frozen corn
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste
- sour cream and cilantro for serving

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Heat oil in a skillet. Cook onion 5 minutes. Add garlic, cumin, and chili powder. Cook 1 minute.
3. Add black beans and corn. Season and cook 3 minutes.
4. Spread 1/2 cup enchilada sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish.
5. Fill each tortilla with the bean mixture and a sprinkle of cheese. Roll and place seam-side down.
6. Pour remaining sauce over enchiladas and top with remaining cheese.
7. Cover with foil and bake 20 minutes. Uncover and bake 10 more minutes.

## Notes

- Assemble the night before and refrigerate - bake straight from the fridge adding 10 minutes
- Add sauteed bell peppers or zucchini to the filling
- Hatch green chile in the sauce makes this exceptional in late summer
