# Summary

Sticky Toffee Pudding from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 100g Butter
- 175g Muscovado Sugar
- 2 large Eggs
- 225g Self-raising Flour
- 1 tsp Baking Powder
- 1 tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda
- 3 tbs Black Treacle
- 275ml Milk
- to serve Double Cream
- 100g Butter
- 125g Muscovado Sugar
- 1 tbs Black Treacle
- 300ml Double Cream
- 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 180C/160C Fan/Gas 4.
2. Butter a wide shallow 1.7-litre/3-pint ovenproof dish.
3. Put the butter, sugar, eggs, flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and treacle into a mixing bowl.
4. Beat using an electric handheld whisk for about 30 seconds or until combined.
5. Pour in the milk gradually and whisk again until smooth.
6. Pour into the prepared dish.
7. Bake for 35–40 minutes or until well risen and springy in the centre.
8. To make the sauce, put all the ingredients into a saucepan and stir over a low heat until the sugar has dissolved and the butter has melted.
9. Bring to the boil, stirring for a minute.
10. To serve, pour half the sauce over the pudding in the baking dish.
11. Serve with the cream or ice cream.
