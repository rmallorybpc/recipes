# Summary

Blueberry & lemon friands from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 100g Unsalted Butter
- 125g Icing Sugar
- 25g Flour
- 85g Almonds
- 3 Egg White
- 1 Unwaxed Lemon
- 85g Blackberries

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to fan 180C/conventional 200C/gas 6.
2. Generously butter six non-stick friand or muffin tins.
3. Melt the butter and set aside to cool.
4. Sift the icing sugar and flour into a bowl.
5. Add the almonds and mix everything between your fingers.
6. Whisk the egg whites in another bowl until they form a light, floppy foam.
7. Make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients, tip in the egg whites and lemon rind, then lightly stir in the butter to form a soft batter.
8. Divide the batter among the tins, a large serving spoon is perfect for this job.
9. Sprinkle a handful of blueberries over each cake and bake for 15-20 minutes until just firm to the touch and golden brown.
10. Cool in the tins for 5 minutes, then turn out and cool on a wire rack.
11. To serve, dust lightly with icing sugar.
