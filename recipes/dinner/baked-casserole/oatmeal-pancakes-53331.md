# Summary

Oatmeal pancakes from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 1 medium Egg
- 80 ml Milk
- 1 tablespoon Sugar
- 1 tsp Baking Powder
- Pinch Salt
- 1 dash Vanilla Extract
- 150g Ground Oats
- 150g Butter
- 1 tablespoon Sugar Syrup
- 3 Strawberries

## Instructions

1. Place all the ingredients in the glass and beat.
2. Let the mixture stand for 10 minutes.
3. Grease a hot frying pan with a little butter and pour a little of the mixture.
4. When it starts to bubble on the surface, turn over with a spatula.
5. Cook over medium-low heat so that they do not burn.
6. Finally, add the caramel and strawberries.
