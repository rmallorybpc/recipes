# Summary

Chocolate churros with chocolate & salted caramel sauce from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- For frying Sunflower Oil
- To serve Ice Cream
- 120g Muscovado Sugar
- 60g Butter
- 250ml Double Cream
- 100g Dark Chocolate
- 100g Golden Caster Sugar
- 2 teaspoon Cinnamon
- 200g Plain Flour
- 50g Cocoa Powder
- 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
- 50g Melted Butter

## Instructions

1. step 1.
2. To make the sauce, tip the sugar and butter into a saucepan and bring to a simmer until the sugar has melted, then stir in the cream and simmer until you have a smooth sauce, about 2-3 mins.
3. Remove from the heat, stir through a pinch of sea salt and the chocolate, and continue to stir for 2-3 mins, or until it has completely melted.
4. Keep warm or leave to cool for reheating later.
5. For the coating, mix the sugar with the cinnamon and a small pinch of fine sea salt and set aside.
6. step 2.
7. For the churros, tip the flour, cocoa, baking powder and a large pinch of salt into a bowl.
8. Add the vanilla extract and melted butter, then carefully pour in 250-300ml boiling water and whisk to make a smooth, very thick batter.
9. Leave to cool for a few minutes, then scrape the mixture into a piping bag fitted with a medium star nozzle.
10. step 3.
11. Tip sunflower oil into a deep-fat fryer following the manufacturer’s instructions, or a heavy-based pan, ensuring it is no more than a third full.
12. Heat to 170C, or until a cube of bread dropped in browns in 30 seconds.
13. Pipe 12-15cm lengths of dough into the oil, snipping off the end of each one with a pair of scissors as you go.
14. Pipe four or five at a time and cook for around 4 mins, or until golden and crisp, turning them with tongs or a slotted spoon as they cook.
15. Remove from the oil and place on a wire rack.
16. Repeat with the rest of the dough.
17. Leave to cool if reheating later, otherwise roll the churros in the cinnamon sugar and serve immediately with the warm chocolate sauce and ice cream.
18. To reheat the churros, put on a tray and bake for 5-8 mins, then roll in the sugar coating.
19. Meanwhile, warm the sauce in a pan and serve alongside for dipping.
