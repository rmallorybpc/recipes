# Summary

Peanut Butter Cookies from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Peanut Butter
- 1/2 cup Sugar
- 1 Egg

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350ºF (180ºC).
2. In a large bowl, mix together the peanut butter, sugar, and egg.
3. Scoop out a spoonful of dough and roll it into a ball.
4. Place the cookie balls onto a nonstick baking sheet.
5. For extra decoration and to make them cook more evenly, flatten the cookie balls by pressing a fork down on top of them, then press it down again at a 90º angle to make a criss-cross pattern.
6. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the bottom of the cookies are golden brown.
7. Remove from baking sheet and cool.
8. Enjoy!.
