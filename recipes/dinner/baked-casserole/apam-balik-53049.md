# Summary

Apam balik from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 200ml Milk
- 60ml Oil
- 2 Eggs
- 1600g Flour
- 3 tsp Baking Powder
- 1/2 tsp Salt
- 25g Unsalted Butter
- 45g Sugar
- 3 tbs Peanut Butter

## Instructions

1. Mix milk, oil and egg together.
2. Sift flour, baking powder and salt into the mixture.
3. Stir well until all ingredients are combined evenly.
4. Spread some batter onto the pan.
5. Spread a thin layer of batter to the side of the pan.
6. Cover the pan for 30-60 seconds until small air bubbles appear.
7. Add butter, cream corn, crushed peanuts and sugar onto the pancake.
8. Fold the pancake into half once the bottom surface is browned.
9. Cut into wedges and best eaten when it is warm.
