# Summary

Sesame Cucumber Salad from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 2 medium Cucumber
- 1/2 tsp Salt
- 2 tsp Sesame Seed Oil
- 2 tablespoons Rice Vinegar
- 6 Basil Leaves
- 1/2 tsp Red Chilli Flakes
- 2 tsp Sesame Seed

## Instructions

1. Prep the cucumbers:.
2. Peel the cucumbers.
3. Cut them into quarters, lengthwise. (If the seeds are bitter, scrape out the seeds and discard.) Cut the cucumbers again, crosswise, into 1/2-inch thick pieces.
4. Toss the salad:.
5. Place cucumbers into a serving bowl.
6. Sprinkle with salt.
7. Toss with sesame oil, seasoned rice vinegar, basil (if using), and chili flakes.
8. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds if using.
