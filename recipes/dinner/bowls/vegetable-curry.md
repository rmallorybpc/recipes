## Ingredients

- 2 cans chickpeas (15 oz each), drained
- 1 can coconut milk (13.5 oz)
- 1 can crushed tomatoes (14 oz)
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
- 2 cups spinach or kale
- 1 large sweet potato, cubed
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- salt to taste
- fresh cilantro for garnish
- basmati rice or naan for serving

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and cook 5 minutes.
2. Add garlic and ginger. Cook 1 minute more.
3. Stir in curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and cayenne. Cook 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add diced tomatoes, coconut milk, sweet potato, and chickpeas.
5. Simmer 20-25 minutes until sweet potato is tender.
6. Stir in spinach and cook 2 minutes until wilted.
7. Season with salt and serve over rice with naan.

## Notes

- Add a squeeze of lemon juice at the end to brighten the flavors
- Swap sweet potato for butternut squash in fall
- Freezes beautifully for up to 3 months
