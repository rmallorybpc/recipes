# Summary

Kumpir from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 2 large Potatoes
- 2 tbs Butter
- 150g Cheese
- 1 large Onion
- 1 large Red Pepper
- Pinch Red Chilli Flakes

## Instructions

1. If you order kumpir in Turkey, the standard filling is first, lots of butter mashed into the potato, followed by cheese.
2. There’s then a row of other toppings that you can just point at to your heart’s content – sweetcorn, olives, salami, coleslaw, Russian salad, allsorts – and you walk away with an over-stuffed potato because you got ever-excited by the choices on offer.
3. Grate (roughly – you can use as much as you like) 150g of cheese.
4. Finely chop one onion and one sweet red pepper.
5. Put these ingredients into a large bowl with a good sprinkling of salt and pepper, chilli flakes (optional).
