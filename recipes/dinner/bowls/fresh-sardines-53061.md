# Summary

Fresh sardines from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 500g Sardines
- Dash Vegetable Oil
- To Glaze Flour
- Dash Salt

## Instructions

1. Wash the fish under the cold tap.
2. Roll in the flour and deep fry in oil until crispy.
3. Lay on kitchen towel to get rid of the excess oil and serve hot or cold with a slice of lemon.
