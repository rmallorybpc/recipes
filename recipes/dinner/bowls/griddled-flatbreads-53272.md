# Summary

Griddled flatbreads from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 250g Strong Wholemeal Flour
- 250g Strong white bread flour
- 15g Yeast
- 1 tsp Sugar
- 2 tablespoons Olive Oil

## Instructions

1. step 1.
2. Tip the flours into a food processor.
3. Add the yeast, sugar and 1tsp salt, then mix well.
4. Pour in 350ml warm water and the oil, then process to a soft dough.
5. Mix for 1 min, then leave until doubled in size, about 1 hour.
6. step 2.
7. Pulse the dough a couple of times just to knock out the air, then tip onto a floured surface.
8. Cut the dough in half and roll out one half to a rectangle about 20 x 40cm.
9. Trim the edges using a large sharp knife, then cut into eight 10cm squares.
10. Line a large tray or two baking sheets with non-stick paper and arrange the bread rectangles over the tray in one layer.
11. Repeat with the other half of the dough.
12. Leave in a warm place for about 30 mins until the dough is just starting to rise.
13. step 3.
14. Place bread directly onto the BBQ racks and cook for a couple of mins until they puff up, then flip over and cook on the other side.
15. Tip into a basket and serve with the dips.
