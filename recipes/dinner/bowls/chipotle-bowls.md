## Ingredients

- 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
- 2 cups long-grain white rice
- 2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 cups frozen corn, thawed
- 1 can diced tomatoes with green chiles
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
- 1 tablespoon adobo sauce
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- juice of 2 limes
- salt and pepper to taste
- sour cream, salsa, guacamole, shredded cheese, and cilantro for serving

## Instructions

1. Season chicken with chipotle, adobo sauce, cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
2. Cook chicken in olive oil over medium-high heat 6-7 minutes per side. Rest and slice.
3. Cook rice according to package directions. Stir in lime juice and cilantro.
4. Warm black beans with cumin and salt.
5. Build bowls with rice, chicken, beans, corn, tomatoes, and desired toppings.

## Notes

- Marinate chicken overnight for deeper flavor
- This is excellent for meal prep — components keep separately for 4 days
- Add pickled jalapeños for extra heat
