# Summary

## Ingredients

- 200g Chickpea Flour
- 600ml Water
- 4 tablespoons Olive Oil
- To taste Salt
- To taste Pepper

## Instructions

1. Prepare the Batter: Whisk together chickpea flour, water, salt, and pepper.
2. Let sit for at least 4 hours.
3. Bake: Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
4. Pour olive oil into a round baking dish and heat in the oven.
5. Pour in the batter and bake for 25-30 minutes, until golden.
6. Serve: Slice and serve hot, optionally with black pepper on top.
7. Pro Tips:.
8. Let the batter rest for at least 2 hours, or overnight in the refrigerator, to ensure the chickpea flour fully hydrates and the flavors meld.
9. For a crispy edge, preheat the baking pan with oil in the oven before adding the batter.
