# Summary

Locro from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 500g Dried white corn
- 200g Dried white beans
- 300g Pumpkin
- 200g Potatoes
- 300g Beef
- 200g Pork
- 1 Chorizo
- 1 chopped Onion
- 1 teaspoon Paprika
- To taste Salt
- To taste Pepper

## Instructions

1. Soak: Soak corn and beans overnight in water.
2. Cook Meats: In a large pot, brown the beef and pork.
3. Add onions and spices, cooking until translucent.
4. Simmer the Stew: Add soaked corn and beans, pumpkin, potato, and enough water to cover.
5. Simmer for 2-3 hours, until thick.
6. Serve: Enjoy hot, with bread on the side.
7. Pro Tips:.
8. Toasting the corn slightly before adding it to the stew enhances its flavor.
9. Add a spoonful of paprika or a dash of cumin for an extra layer of warmth and complexity.
