## Ingredients

- 12 lasagne noodles
- 3 medium zucchini, thinly sliced lengthwise
- 2 yellow squash, thinly sliced lengthwise
- 2 cups ricotta cheese
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups marinara sauce
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 large onion, diced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- salt and pepper to taste
- fresh basil for garnish

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Cook noodles al dente.
2. Salt zucchini and squash slices. Let sit 15 minutes. Pat dry.
3. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil 5 minutes. Add squash and cook 4 minutes.
4. Mix ricotta with eggs, half the Parmesan, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
5. Layer in a 9x13 dish: sauce, noodles, ricotta, vegetables, mozzarella. Repeat.
6. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
7. Cover and bake 30 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 more minutes.
8. Rest 15 minutes before cutting.

## Notes

- Salting and drying the squash removes excess water that would make the lasagna soggy
- Colorado zucchini in July and August is exceptional here
- This is an excellent make-ahead dish — assemble day before and refrigerate
