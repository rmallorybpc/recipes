## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 8 oz egg noodles
- 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups beef broth
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup (10.5 oz)
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- salt and pepper to taste
- fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions

1. Cook egg noodles according to package directions. Drain.
2. Brown ground beef in a large skillet. Drain fat. Remove and set aside.
3. Cook onion and mushrooms in butter 7 minutes until golden.
4. Add garlic and flour. Cook 1 minute.
5. Add broth, cream of mushroom soup, and Worcestershire. Stir until smooth.
6. Return beef. Simmer 10 minutes.
7. Remove from heat and stir in sour cream.
8. Serve over egg noodles with parsley.

## Notes

- Do not boil after adding sour cream
- Ground turkey works equally well
- Add a teaspoon of Dijon mustard for extra depth
