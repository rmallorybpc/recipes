## Ingredients

- 20 oz refrigerated cheese tortellini
- 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 3 cups baby spinach
- 1/2 cup basil pesto
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- salt and pepper to taste
- fresh basil for garnish

## Instructions

1. Cook tortellini according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water. Drain.
2. Season shrimp with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
3. Heat oil in a skillet over high heat. Cook shrimp 2 minutes per side until pink. Remove.
4. Add garlic to the skillet. Cook 30 seconds.
5. Add spinach and toss until wilted.
6. Add tortellini, pesto, and lemon juice. Toss with pasta water to loosen.
7. Add shrimp back. Toss everything together.
8. Serve with Parmesan and fresh basil.

## Notes

- Refrigerated tortellini cooks in 3 minutes — do not overcook
- Store-bought pesto works perfectly here
- Add sun-dried tomatoes for extra flavor
