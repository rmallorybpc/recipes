## Ingredients

- 1 lb spaghetti
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1/2 lb ground pork (optional)
- 1 large onion, finely diced
- 3 carrots, finely diced
- 3 stalks celery, finely diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
- 1/2 cup dry red wine
- 1/2 cup whole milk
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- salt and pepper to taste
- grated Parmesan cheese for serving

## Instructions

1. Cook onion, carrot, and celery in olive oil 8 minutes until soft.
2. Add garlic and cook 1 minute. Add ground beef and pork. Brown thoroughly.
3. Add tomato paste. Cook 2 minutes.
4. Pour in wine. Simmer 5 minutes.
5. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
6. Simmer 30 minutes. Stir in milk and simmer 10 more minutes.
7. Cook spaghetti al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
8. Toss pasta with sauce, adding pasta water as needed.
9. Serve with Parmesan.

## Notes

- The longer bolognese simmers the better — 2 hours is ideal
- Milk rounds out the acidity of the tomatoes
- Use the pasta cooking water to loosen the sauce — it binds everything together
