## Ingredients

- 12 oz pasta (penne or rigatoni)
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, torn into pieces
- 1/2 cup fresh basil, roughly torn
- 3 tablespoons good olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- salt and pepper to taste
- balsamic glaze for drizzling

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining.
2. While pasta cooks, combine cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, olive oil, garlic, and red pepper flakes in a large bowl.
3. Drain pasta and immediately add to the bowl. Toss vigorously.
4. Add a splash of pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and serve immediately.

## Notes

- Quality ingredients matter most here
- Cherry tomatoes from a Colorado farmers market in July and August are exceptional
- Serve immediately - this does not hold well as leftovers
