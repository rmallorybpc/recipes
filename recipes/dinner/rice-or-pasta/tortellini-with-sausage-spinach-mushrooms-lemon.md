## Ingredients

- 20 oz refrigerated cheese tortellini
- 1 lb Italian sausage, casings removed
- 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
- 3 cups baby spinach
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- juice of 1 lemon
- zest of 1 lemon
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- salt and pepper to taste
- fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions

1. Cook tortellini according to package directions. Drain.
2. Brown sausage in olive oil, breaking it up. Remove.
3. Cook mushrooms in the same pan 5 minutes until golden.
4. Add garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook 30 seconds.
5. Add cream, broth, lemon juice, and zest. Simmer 3 minutes.
6. Add spinach and toss until wilted.
7. Add tortellini and sausage back. Toss to coat.
8. Serve with Parmesan and parsley.

## Notes

- The lemon brightens the richness of the cream and sausage
- Hot Italian sausage adds more flavor than sweet
- Add white wine instead of broth for extra depth
