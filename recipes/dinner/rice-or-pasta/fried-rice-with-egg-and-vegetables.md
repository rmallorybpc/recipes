## Ingredients

- 1 lb Chicken Thighs
- 1 tsp Salt
- 3  tablespoons Canola Oil
- 3 Large Egg
- 2/3 Cup Onion
- 2 cloves minced Garlic
- 2 tsp Ground Ginger
- 1 large Carrots
- 2/3 Cup Peas
- 4 cups Jasmine Rice
- 2 sliced Scallions
- 1/2 tsp Chinese five spice powder
- 2.5 tbsp Soy Sauce
- 1 tsp Sesame Seed Oil

## Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok over high heat.
2. Add carrots and stir fry 2 minutes. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds. Add peas.
3. Push vegetables to the side. Add beaten eggs and scramble until just set.
4. Add cold rice, breaking up any clumps. Stir fry 2-3 minutes until heated through.
5. Season with soy sauce and sesame oil. Toss everything together.
6. Finish with scallions and serve immediately.

## Notes

- 15 minutes start to finish - perfect for Monday and Wednesday time caps
- Day-old refrigerated rice is essential
- Works with any protein - add leftover chicken, shrimp, or tofu
