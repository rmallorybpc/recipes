# Summary

Thai-style fish broth with greens from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 100g Brown Rice Noodle
- 500ml Chicken Stock
- 1 tblsp Thai Red Curry Paste
- 4 Lime Leaves
- 1 tblsp Fish Sauce
- 200g White Fish
- 100g Raw King Prawns
- 2 Pak Choi
- Handful Coriander Leaves

## Instructions

1. step 1.
2. Cook the noodles following pack instructions.
3. Refresh in cold water and drain well.
4. step 2.
5. Put the stock in a large saucepan and stir in the curry paste, lime leaves, fish sauce and 250ml cold water.
6. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 mins.
7. step 3.
8. Cut the fish into roughly 3cm cubes and add to the pan.
9. Return to a simmer, then cook for 2 mins uncovered.
10. step 4.
11. Stir in the noodles, prawns and pak choi, and simmer for 2-3 mins or until the fish and prawns are just cooked.
12. Serve in bowls scattered with coriander.
