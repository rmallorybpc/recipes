## Ingredients

- 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
- 1 lb asparagus, trimmed
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- juice of 1 lemon
- zest of 1 lemon
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme or dill
- salt and pepper to taste
- lemon slices for serving

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Arrange asparagus on the baking sheet and toss with 2 tablespoons olive oil, salt, and pepper.
3. Nestle salmon fillets among the asparagus.
4. Mix remaining olive oil with garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, and thyme. Spoon over salmon.
5. Season salmon with salt and pepper.
6. Roast 15-18 minutes until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
7. Serve with lemon slices.

## Notes

- Asparagus is at peak in Colorado in May and June
- Swap thyme for fresh dill for a more classic flavor pairing
- Works with any thick-cut fish fillet
