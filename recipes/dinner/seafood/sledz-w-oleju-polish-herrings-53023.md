# Summary

Sledz w Oleju (Polish Herrings) from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 8 Herring
- 1 sliced Onion
- 1/2 cup Olive Oil
- 1 tsp Allspice
- 1 tsp Pepper
- 1 Bay Leaf

## Instructions

1. Soak herring in cold water for at least 1 hour.
2. If very salty, repeat, changing the water each time.
3. Drain thoroughly and slice herring into bite-size pieces.
4. Place in a jar large enough to accommodate the pieces and cover with oil, allspice, peppercorns, and bay leaf.
5. Close the jar.
6. Refrigerate for 2 to 3 days before eating.
7. This will keep refrigerated up to 2 weeks.
8. Serve with finely chopped onion or onion slices, lemon, and parsley or dill.
