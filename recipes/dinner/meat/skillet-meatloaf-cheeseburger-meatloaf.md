## Ingredients

- 2 lbs ground beef (80/20)
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup whole milk
- 1 large onion, finely diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1/4 cup ketchup mixed with 1 tablespoon brown sugar for glaze

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Soak breadcrumbs in milk 5 minutes.
3. Mix ground beef with soaked breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, eggs, Worcestershire, ketchup, mustard, cheese, and spices.
4. Form into a loaf in a 9x5 loaf pan or free-form in a skillet.
5. Spread ketchup-brown sugar glaze over the top.
6. Bake 55-60 minutes until internal temperature reaches 160°F.
7. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.

## Notes

- Do not overmix — it makes meatloaf dense
- The milk-soaked breadcrumbs keep it moist
- Leftovers make exceptional sandwiches
