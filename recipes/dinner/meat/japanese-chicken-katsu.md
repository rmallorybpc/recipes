## Ingredients

- 4 chicken breasts, pounded to 1/2-inch thickness
- 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1/2 cup flour
- salt and pepper
- vegetable oil for shallow frying
- 2 cups steamed rice
- 2 cups shredded cabbage
- 4 tablespoons tonkatsu sauce
- Japanese mayo for drizzling (optional)

## Instructions

1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Set up a breading station: flour, beaten egg, panko.
2. Dredge each cutlet in flour, then egg, then panko. Press panko firmly to adhere.
3. Heat 1/2 inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Fry chicken 4-5 minutes per side until deep golden and cooked through. Work in batches.
5. Drain on paper towels and slice into strips.
6. Serve over rice with shredded cabbage drizzled with tonkatsu sauce and mayo.

## Notes

- Panko breadcrumbs are essential for the crunch
- Pound the chicken evenly so it cooks at a uniform rate
- Tonkatsu sauce is available at King Soopers in the Asian foods aisle
