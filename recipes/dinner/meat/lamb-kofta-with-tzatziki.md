## Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs ground lamb
- 1 small onion, grated
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, minced
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne
- salt and pepper
- 4 pita breads
- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cucumber, grated and squeezed dry
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon fresh dill
- cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion for serving

## Instructions

1. Combine lamb with grated onion, garlic, parsley, and all spices. Mix well and season.
2. Form into oval patties or mold around skewers.
3. Make tzatziki by combining yogurt, grated cucumber, lemon juice, garlic, and dill. Season with salt.
4. Grill kofta over high heat 3-4 minutes per side until cooked through and charred.
5. Warm pita directly on the grill.
6. Serve kofta with tzatziki, pita, and chopped tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.

## Notes

- Lamb is traditional but ground beef works perfectly
- Soak wooden skewers in water 30 minutes before grilling
- Excellent for summer grilling when Colorado tomatoes are at peak
