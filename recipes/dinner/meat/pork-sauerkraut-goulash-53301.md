# Summary

Pork & sauerkraut goulash from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 3  tablespoons Lard
- 4 Chopped Onion
- 1 tablespoon Cumin Seeds
- 800g Pork Shoulder
- 4 Cloves Crushed Garlic
- 2 tablespoons Plain Flour
- 2 tablespoons Paprika
- 1 1/2 L Beef Stock
- 4 Bay Leaves
- 400g White Sauerkraut
- 200ml Whipping Cream
- 4 tablespoons Sour Cream

## Instructions

1. step 1.
2. Heat the lard in a saucepan over a medium heat and fry the finely chopped onion until golden, around 5-8 mins.
3. Tip in the cumin and pork, and fry for 10 mins all over until browned.
4. Add the garlic, season well and scatter over the flour.
5. Cook for about a minute, then add the paprika and cook for 1 min more.
6. step 2.
7. Pour in half of the stock, add the bay leaves, and simmer with the lid on over a low heat for 30 mins.
8. step 3.
9. Add the sauerkraut, remaining stock and a drizzle of the sauerkraut pickling juices.
10. Simmer with the lid for 30 mins, stirring occasionally, until the meat is tender.
11. Stir in the cream and simmer for 5 mins to combine the flavours.
12. Season to taste, the serve garnished with a spoonful of soured cream.
