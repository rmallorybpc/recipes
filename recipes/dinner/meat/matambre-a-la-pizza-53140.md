# Summary

Matambre a la Pizza from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 1 kg Beef Flank Steak
- 200g Tomato Sauce
- 200g Mozzarella
- 1 tbsp Oregano
- 100g Green Olives
- To taste Salt
- To taste Pepper

## Instructions

1. Prepare the Steak: Season the steak with salt and pepper.
2. Grill one side until half-cooked.
3. Add Toppings: Spread tomato sauce over the cooked side, then add cheese, oregano, and olives.
4. Finish Grilling: Grill until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
5. Serve: Slice and serve hot.
6. Pro Tips:.
7. Tenderize the matambre by scoring it lightly on both sides.
8. This helps it cook more evenly and absorb the flavors.
9. Precook the matambre on the grill before adding the toppings to ensure it's fully cooked without burning the cheese.
