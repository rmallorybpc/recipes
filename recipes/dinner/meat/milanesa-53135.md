## Ingredients

- 4 Beef Cutlet
- 2 Beaten Egg
- 200g Breadcrumbs
- 2 tbs chopped Parsley
- 2 cloves minced Garlic
- To taste Salt
- To taste Pepper

## Instructions

1. Season the Cutlets: Salt and pepper the cutlets.
2. Bread the Cutlets: Dip each cutlet in egg, then coat with a mixture of breadcrumbs, parsley, and garlic.
3. Fry: Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
4. Fry the cutlets until golden and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per side.
5. Serve: Serve hot with lemon wedges or a side salad.
6. Pro Tips:.
7. For an extra crispy crust, use panko breadcrumbs mixed with finely grated Parmesan cheese.
8. Gently pound the cutlets to an even thickness for uniform cooking.
