## Ingredients

- 3-4 lbs chicken pieces (thighs, drumsticks, breasts)
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon onion powder
- 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- vegetable oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk with hot sauce for at least 4 hours or overnight.
2. Mix flour with all dry spices.
3. Remove chicken from buttermilk and dredge in seasoned flour. Press firmly to adhere.
4. Heat oil to 325°F in a large cast iron skillet or Dutch oven.
5. Fry chicken in batches 12-15 minutes per side until golden and cooked through.
6. Drain on a wire rack. Do not place on paper towels — it steams the crust.

## Notes

- Overnight buttermilk marinade produces the most tender and flavorful chicken
- Maintain oil temperature — too hot burns the crust before chicken is cooked
- Let chicken rest 10 minutes before serving
