# Summary

Mbuzi Choma (Roasted Goat) from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 1 kg Goat Meat
- 1 kg Corn Flour
- 2 Tomatoes
- Pinch Salt
- 1 Onion
- 1 Green Chilli
- 1  bunch Coriander Leaves

## Instructions

1. Steps for the Meat:.
2. Roast meat over medium heat for 50 minutes and salt it as you turn it.
3. Steps for Ugali:.
4. Bring the water and salt to a boil in a heavy-bottomed saucepan.
5. Stir in the cornmeal slowly, letting it fall through the fingers of your hand.
6. Reduce heat to medium-low and continue stirring regularly, smashing any lumps with a spoon, until the mush pulls away from the sides of the pot and becomes very thick, about 10 minutes.
7. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
8. Place the ugali into a large serving bowl.
9. Wet your hands with water, form a ball and serve.
10. Steps for Kachumbari: Mix the tomatoes, onions, chili and coriander leaves in a bowl.
11. Serve and enjoy!.
