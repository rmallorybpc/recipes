# Summary

Carbonada Criolla from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 500g Beef
- 1 chopped Onion
- 2 chopped Carrots
- 2 chopped Potatoes
- 1 small Pumpkin
- 100g Dried Apricots
- 2 Litres Beef Stock
- To taste Salt
- To taste Pepper

## Instructions

1. Brown the Beef: In a large pot, brown the beef cubes.
2. Remove and set aside.
3. Sauté Vegetables: In the same pot, cook the onion until translucent.
4. Add carrots, potatoes, and pumpkin, cooking for a few minutes.
5. Simmer: Return the beef to the pot.
6. Add broth and dried apricots.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Simmer for 1-2 hours, until the beef is tender.
9. Serve: Enjoy hot, with a crusty piece of bread.
10. Pro Tips:.
11. Brown the beef in batches to ensure it gets a good sear, which adds depth to the stew's flavor.
12. Adding the fruits towards the end of cooking preserves their texture and adds a subtle sweetness to the dish.
