# Summary

## Ingredients

- 4 large Potatoes
- 1  bunch Spring Onions
- 100g Plain Flour
- 1 Egg White
- 150ml Milk
- 1 tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda
- 3 tbs Butter
- 2 tbs Vegetable Oil
- 6 Cherry Tomatoes
- 12 Bacon
- 6 Egg

## Instructions

1. STEP 1.
2. Before you start, put your oven on its lowest setting, ready to keep things warm.
3. Peel the potatoes, grate 2 of them, then set aside.
4. Cut the other 2 into large chunks, then boil for 10-15 mins or until tender.
5. Meanwhile, squeeze as much of the liquid from the grated potatoes as you can using a clean tea towel.
6. Mash the boiled potatoes, then mix with the grated potato, spring onions and flour.
7. STEP 2.
8. Whisk the egg white in a large bowl until it holds soft peaks.
9. Fold in the buttermilk, then add the bicarbonate of soda.
10. Fold into the potato mix.
11. STEP 3.
12. Heat a large non-stick frying pan over a medium heat, then add 1 tbsp butter and some of the oil.
13. Drop 3-4 spoonfuls of the potato mixture into the pan, then gently cook for 3-5 mins on each side until golden and crusty.
14. Keep warm on a plate in the oven while you cook the next batch, adding more butter and oil to the pan before you do so.
15. You will get 16 crumpet-size boxty from the mix.
16. Can be made the day ahead, drained on kitchen paper, then reheated in a low oven for 20 mins.
17. STEP 4.
18. Heat the grill to medium and put the tomatoes in a heavy-based pan.
19. Add a good knob of butter and a little oil, then fry for about 5 mins until softened.
20. Grill the bacon, then pile onto a plate and keep warm.
21. Stack up the boxty, bacon and egg, and serve the tomatoes on the side.
