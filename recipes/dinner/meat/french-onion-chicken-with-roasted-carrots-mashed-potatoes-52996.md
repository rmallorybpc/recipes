# Summary

French Onion Chicken with Roasted Carrots & Mashed Potatoes from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 2 Chicken Breasts
- 12 ounces Carrots
- 5 Potatoes
- 1 Onion
- 1 Beef Stock
- 1 1/2 cup Mozzarella
- 2 tbsp Sour Cream
- Butter
- Sugar
- Vegetable Oil
- Salt
- Pepper

## Instructions

1. 1.
2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
3. Wash and dry all produce.
4. Trim, peel, and cut carrots on a diagonal into ¼-inch-thick pieces.
5. Dice potatoes into ½-inch pieces.
6. Halve, peel, and thinly slice onion.
7. 2.
8. Toss carrots on a baking sheet with a drizzle of oil, salt, and pepper.
9. Roast until browned and tender, 15-20 minutes.
10. 3.
11. Meanwhile, place potatoes in a medium pot with enough salted water to cover by 2 inches.
12. Bring to a boil and cook until tender, 12-15 minutes.
13. Drain and return potatoes to pot; cover to keep warm.
14. 4.
15. While potatoes cook, heat a drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
16. Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned and softened, 8-10 minutes.
17. Sprinkle with 1 tsp sugar (2 tsp for 4 servings).
18. Stir in stock concentrate and 2 TBSP water (¼ cup for 4); season with salt and pepper.
19. Cook until jammy, 2-3 minutes more.
20. Turn off heat; transfer to a small bowl.
21. Wash out pan.
22. 5.
23. Pat chicken dry with paper towels; season all over with salt and pepper.
24. Heat a drizzle of oil in pan used for onion over medium-high heat.
25. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, 5-6 minutes per side.
26. In the last 1-2 minutes of cooking, top with caramelized onion and cheese.
27. Cover pan until cheese melts. (If your pan doesn’t have a lid, cover with a baking sheet!).
28. 6.
29. Heat pot with drained potatoes over low heat; mash with sour cream, 2 TBSP butter (4 TBSP for 4 servings), salt, pepper, and a splash of water (or milk, for extra richness) until smooth.
30. Divide chicken, roasted carrots, and mashed potatoes between plates.
