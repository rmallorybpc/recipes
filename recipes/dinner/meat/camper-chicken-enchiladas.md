## Ingredients

- 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
- 10 flour tortillas
- 2 cans red enchilada sauce (10 oz each)
- 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 can diced green chiles (4 oz)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste
- sour cream and cilantro for serving

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Cook onion and garlic in olive oil 5 minutes. Add green chiles, cumin, and chili powder.
3. Mix with shredded chicken. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Spread 1/2 cup enchilada sauce in a 9x13 baking dish.
5. Fill each tortilla with chicken mixture and a sprinkle of cheese. Roll and place seam-side down.
6. Pour remaining sauce over enchiladas and top with remaining cheese.
7. Cover and bake 20 minutes. Uncover and bake 10 more minutes.
8. Serve with sour cream and cilantro.

## Notes

- Use rotisserie chicken for maximum ease
- Assemble the night before and refrigerate — bake day-of
- Add black beans to the filling for extra substance
