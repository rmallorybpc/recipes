## Ingredients

- 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 4 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
- 2 medium tomatoes, sliced
- 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
- 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- salt and pepper to taste
- toothpicks for securing

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Cut a pocket into each chicken breast by slicing horizontally, being careful not to cut all the way through.
3. Season inside and out with salt and pepper.
4. Stuff each pocket with mozzarella, tomato, and basil. Secure with toothpicks.
5. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken 3 minutes per side until golden.
6. Transfer skillet to oven and bake 15-18 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
7. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and serve.

## Notes

- Tomatoes from a Colorado farmers market in July and August make this exceptional
- Pound the chicken slightly if it is very thick
- Sun-dried tomatoes work great as a year-round alternative
