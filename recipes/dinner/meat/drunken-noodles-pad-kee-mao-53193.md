# Summary

Drunken noodles (pad kee mao) from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 200g Flat Rice Noodles
- 2 tblsp Vegetable Oil
- 3 cloves Chopped Garlic
- 3 chopped Birds-eye Chillies
- 225g Sirloin steak
- 100g Broccoli
- Handful Basil Leaves
- 4 tbsp Soy Sauce
- 4 tbsp Oyster Sauce
- 1 teaspoon Fish Sauce
- 1 tblsp Sugar

## Instructions

1. step 1.
2. Prepare the noodles following pack instructions, then drain and set aside.
3. Combine all the ingredients for the sauce in a small bowl or jug, and set aside.
4. step 2.
5. Heat the oil in a large wok or frying pan over a high heat and stir-fry the garlic and chilli for 20 seconds until just starting to turn golden.
6. Add the steak and stir-fry for 1 minute until seared and starting to brown.
7. Add the broccoli and stir-fry for another minute, then tip in the noodles and add the sauce.
8. Stir-fry for another 1-2 mins until well combined and piping hot.
9. step 3.
10. Stir through the basil and turn off the heat.
11. Serve immediately to avoid overcooking.
