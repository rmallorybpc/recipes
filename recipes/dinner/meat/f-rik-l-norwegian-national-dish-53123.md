# Summary

Fårikål (Norwegian National Dish) from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 3 Lbs Lamb
- 1 head White Cabbage
- 3  tablespoons Whole black peppercorns
- 3 tsp Salt
- 1 1/2 cups Water
- 5 tablespoons Flour

## Instructions

1. ▢.
2. Cut the lamb into large pieces.
3. Slice the cabbage into large wedges, keeping the core attached.
4. Add a layer of lamb pieces to the bottom of a large pot, fatty side down.
5. Sprinkle with peppercorns and salt.
6. Add a layer of cabbage wedges on top.
7. Repeat with more layers of lamb, peppercorns, and cabbage, ending with cabbage on top.
8. Optional: Sprinkle a couple of tablespoons on top of the lamb for a thicker stew.
9. Add water to the pot and bring to a boil.
10. Cover and reduce heat.
11. Cook on low heat for 2 – 3 hours, until the lamb gently falls away from the bone.
12. Serve with boiled potatoes and fresh parsley, covering generously with the fårikål broth.
