## Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs ground beef (85% lean)
- 8 small corn or flour tortillas
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- salt to taste
- juice of 1 lime
- shredded cheese, shredded lettuce, diced tomato, sour cream, and salsa for serving

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook onion 4 minutes until softened.
2. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
3. Add ground beef and cook, breaking up, until browned. Drain excess fat.
4. Add all spices, tomato paste, and water. Stir and simmer 3 minutes until sauce thickens.
5. Squeeze lime juice over the meat.
6. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet.
7. Fill with meat and desired toppings.

## Notes

- The lime juice at the end brightens everything
- Make a double batch of the seasoning to keep on hand
- Leftover taco meat is great in burritos or on nachos the next day
