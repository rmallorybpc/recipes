# Summary

Slow-roasted ham with lemon, garlic & sage from TheMealDB imported into the planner collection.

## Ingredients

- 5 chopped cloves Garlic
- Zest of 1 Lemon
- 15g Salt
- 10g Black Pepper
- 50 ml Vegetable Oil
- 2kg Pork Shoulder
- 500ml Dry White Wine

## Instructions

1. step 1.
2. Heat the oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6.
3. Bash the garlic, sage, lemon zest, salt and pepper together using a pestle and mortar until the mixture becomes a paste.
4. Stir in the oil, then spread the mixture over the pork shoulder, avoiding the skin on top.
5. Score the skin using a sharp knife, then rub a large pinch of salt into the skin.
6. Tie the pork together using kitchen string.
7. step 2.
8. Line a large baking tray with a double layer of foil and put the pork on top, skin-side up.
9. Bring the sides of the foil up around the pork to create a parcel, then pour the wine into the tray around the sides.
10. Transfer to the oven and reduce the temperature to 140C/120C fan/gas 1.
11. Roast for 4-5 hrs, or until a meat thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the pork reads 70C.
12. step 3.
13. Turn the oven up to 240C/220C fan/gas 9.
14. Carefully spoon the pork roasting juices from the pan into a saucepan and cook over a medium heat for 10-15 mins, or until reduced by a third.
15. Season to taste.
16. step 4.
17. Meanwhile, arrange the foil around the meat so only the skin is exposed, then return to the oven for 10-15 mins until the skin is puffed up and browned all over.
18. Leave to rest for 20 mins before slicing.
19. Serve with the sauce drizzled over.
