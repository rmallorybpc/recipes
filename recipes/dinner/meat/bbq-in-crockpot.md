## Ingredients

- 3-4 lbs pork shoulder or pork butt
- 1 1/2 cups BBQ sauce
- 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- hamburger buns and coleslaw for serving

## Instructions

1. Mix all spices and rub over pork shoulder.
2. Place pork in slow cooker. Pour in BBQ sauce, vinegar, and broth.
3. Cook on low 8-10 hours or high 4-5 hours until fork-tender.
4. Remove pork and shred with two forks.
5. Return shredded pork to the slow cooker and stir into the juices.
6. Serve on buns with coleslaw.

## Notes

- The longer it cooks the more tender it gets
- Excellent for meal prep — keeps refrigerated for 5 days
- Also great on nachos, baked potatoes, or pizza
