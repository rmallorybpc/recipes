## Ingredients

- 1 tbls Olive Oil
- 1 Onions
- 1 clove Garlic
- 1 tbsp Butter
- 250g Mushrooms
- 500g Beef Fillet
- 1tbsp Plain Flour
- 150g Creme Fraiche
- 1 tbsp English Mustard
- 100ml Beef Stock
- Topping Parsley

## Instructions

1. Season steak slices with salt and pepper. Toss with flour.
2. Brown steak in oil over high heat. Remove and set aside.
3. Cook onion and mushrooms in butter 8 minutes until golden.
4. Add garlic, tomato paste, and Worcestershire. Cook 1 minute.
5. Add broth and scrape up browned bits. Return steak.
6. Simmer 15 minutes until tender.
7. Remove from heat and stir in sour cream.
8. Serve over egg noodles with parsley.

## Notes

- Do not boil after adding sour cream — it will curdle
- Round steak is tougher than ribeye and benefits from longer simmering
- Ground beef can substitute for a faster version
