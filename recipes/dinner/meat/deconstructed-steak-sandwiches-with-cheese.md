## Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs ribeye or sirloin steak, thinly sliced
- 4 hoagie rolls or sub rolls
- 1 large onion, thinly sliced
- 2 green bell peppers, thinly sliced
- 8 slices provolone or white American cheese
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- salt and pepper to taste
- mushrooms, sliced (optional)

## Instructions

1. Cook onions and peppers in butter over medium heat 10 minutes until caramelized.
2. Season steak slices with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Cook steak in oil over high heat 2 minutes until browned. Add Worcestershire sauce.
4. Combine steak and vegetables. Top with cheese slices and cover until melted.
5. Toast rolls in butter.
6. Serve steak and cheese mixture over or alongside toasted rolls.

## Notes

- Slice steak as thin as possible — partially freeze for easier slicing
- White American cheese melts best for authentic Philly flavor
- Add hot cherry peppers for a classic Philadelphia touch
