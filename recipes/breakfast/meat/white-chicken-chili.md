## Ingredients

- 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 2 cans white beans (cannellini or Great Northern), drained
- 2 cans chicken broth (14.5 oz each)
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cans diced green chiles (4 oz each)
- 1 cup frozen corn
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 4 oz cream cheese, softened
- salt and pepper to taste
- shredded Monterey Jack cheese, cilantro, and lime for serving

## Instructions

1. Add chicken, broth, onion, garlic, green chiles, corn, and spices to a pot. Bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
3. Remove chicken and shred with two forks. Return to pot.
4. Add white beans, sour cream, and cream cheese. Stir until cream cheese melts.
5. Simmer 10 more minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Serve topped with cheese, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.

## Notes

- This works perfectly in a slow cooker on low for 6-8 hours
- Add a diced jalapeño for heat
- Freezes well without the dairy — add sour cream and cream cheese after reheating
