## Ingredients

- 2 slices sourdough or thick-cut bread, toasted
- 2 ripe avocados
- 2 eggs
- juice of half a lemon
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1 tablespoon everything bagel seasoning
- 1 tablespoon butter for frying eggs
- salt and pepper to taste
- microgreens or arugula for topping (optional)

## Instructions

1. Toast bread until golden and crispy.
2. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and red pepper flakes.
3. Fry eggs in butter over medium heat to your preference.
4. Spread avocado generously on toast.
5. Top with fried egg and everything bagel seasoning.
6. Add microgreens if using.

## Notes

- Everything bagel seasoning is available at King Soopers
- Add smoked salmon for a more indulgent version
- Ripe avocados are essential - check by pressing gently near the stem
