## Ingredients

- 1 large Banana
- 2 medium Eggs
- pinch Baking Powder
- spinkling Vanilla Extract
- 1 tsp Oil
- 25g Pecan Nuts
- 125g Raspberries

## Instructions

1. Blend oats into a coarse flour, or use rolled oats as-is for a heartier texture.
2. Mash bananas well in a large bowl.
3. Add eggs, milk, vanilla, and mix. Stir in oat flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
4. Let batter rest 5 minutes.
5. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake.
6. Cook until bubbles form on top and edges look set, about 3 minutes. Flip and cook 2 minutes more.
7. Serve with maple syrup and fresh fruit.

## Notes

- The riper the bananas the sweeter the pancakes
- Blueberries mixed into the batter in summer are exceptional
- Leftover pancakes freeze well - reheat in the toaster
