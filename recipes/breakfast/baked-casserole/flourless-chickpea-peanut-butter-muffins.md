## Ingredients

- 1 can chickpeas (15 oz), drained and rinsed
- 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
- 1/3 cup honey or maple syrup
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a 12-cup muffin tin with liners.
2. Add chickpeas, peanut butter, honey, eggs, and vanilla to a food processor. Blend until completely smooth.
3. Add baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Pulse to combine.
4. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
5. Divide batter evenly among muffin cups.
6. Bake 20-22 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
7. Cool in pan 5 minutes before removing.

## Notes

- Blend the chickpeas very thoroughly — no lumps
- Natural peanut butter with no added sugar works best
- Store refrigerated for up to 5 days
