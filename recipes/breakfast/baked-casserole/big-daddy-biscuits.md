## Ingredients

- 85g Porridge oats
- 85g Desiccated Coconut
- 100g Plain Flour
- 100g Caster Sugar
- 100g Butter
- 1 tblsp Golden Syrup
- 1 teaspoon Bicarbonate Of Soda

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
3. Cut in cold butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
4. Stir in milk just until dough comes together — do not overmix.
5. Turn dough onto a floured surface and pat to 3/4-inch thickness.
6. Cut with a biscuit cutter and place on an ungreased baking sheet.
7. Bake 13-15 minutes until golden brown on top.

## Notes

- Cold butter is essential for flaky layers
- Do not twist the biscuit cutter — press straight down and lift
- Biscuits touching each other in the pan rise taller
