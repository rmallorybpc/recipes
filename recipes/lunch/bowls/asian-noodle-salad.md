## Ingredients

- 1/2 bag Vermicelli Rice Noodles
- 200g Bean Sprouts
- Zest and juice of 2 Lime
- 2-3 tbsp Fish Sauce
- 1 tsp Muscovado Sugar
- 1 sliced Red Onions
- 2 small Lettuce
- 500g Minced Pork
- Knob Ginger
- Pinch Cayenne Pepper
- 1 tsp Sunflower Oil
- 2 tblsp Sesame Seed
- 1 tsp Oil
- 4 Sirloin steak
- 1 sliced Red Chilli

## Instructions

1. Cook rice noodles according to package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
2. Whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey, ginger, garlic, and lime juice.
3. Toss noodles with cabbage, carrots, cucumber, and scallions.
4. Pour dressing over and toss well to combine.
5. Top with cilantro and peanuts.
6. Serve at room temperature or chilled.

## Notes

- Add grilled chicken or shrimp to make it a complete protein meal
- Keeps refrigerated for up to 3 days
- Swap peanuts for edamame for a nut-free version
