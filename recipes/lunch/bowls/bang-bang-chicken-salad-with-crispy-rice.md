# Summary

A short description of the dish.

# Ingredients

- 1 cup uncooked rice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp chili oil
- 5 breaded chicken tenders
- 3 baby cucumbers
- 1 medium carrot
- 1/2 red onion, pickled or regular
- 1 avocado
- 1/4 cup cilantro
- 1/4 cup mayo
- 1/4 cup greek yogurt
- 1/4 cup sweet chili sauce, plus more to toss, if desired
- 1 tbsp sriracha, if desired
- 1 lime, juiced
- sesame seeds

# Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 F.
2. Cook the rice according to the instructions.
3. Once cooled, spread on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
4. Add the olive oil, soy sauce, and chili oil.
5. Mix well and bake for 30-45 minutes, flipping halfway through until the rice is crispy to your liking.
6. While the rice bakes, cook the chicken tenders according to the package instructions.
7. Once done, toss in sweet chili sauce if desired, and cut into bite-sized pieces.
8. Slice the cucumbers into rounds; julienne the carrot; slice the onion into half moons; cube and peel the avocado; mince the cilantro. All all to a large bowl.
9. Make the dressing by mixing the mayo, greek yogurt, sweet chili sauce, sriracha, and lime juice.
10. Add the sliced chicken to the bowl along with the crisped rice.
11. Pour in the dressing and toss.
12. Serve topped with sesame seeds.
