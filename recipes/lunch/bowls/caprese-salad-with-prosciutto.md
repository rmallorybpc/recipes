## Ingredients

- 3 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
- 4 oz prosciutto, thin sliced
- 3 tablespoons good olive oil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
- flaky sea salt and black pepper

## Instructions

1. Arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella on a platter.
2. Tuck basil leaves between the slices.
3. Drape prosciutto across the top.
4. Drizzle generously with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
5. Finish with flaky salt and cracked black pepper.
6. Serve immediately.

## Notes

- This is a no-cook recipe - quality of ingredients matters most
- Heirloom tomatoes from a Colorado farmers market in July and August are exceptional here
- Omit prosciutto for a vegetarian version
